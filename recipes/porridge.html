Porridge


This is a recipe for tasty classic porride

The recipe contains basic ingredients to create a yummy porridge. Porridge is great for breakfast, but can be eaten at any time. Enjoy!


Ingredients
  • Oatmeal
  • Blueberry
  • Honey
  • Milk (Soy or Oat)
  • Nuts (optional)

Steps
  1. Put the oatmeal, blueberries, and milk into into a pot
  2. Heat the pot with the ingredients. But don't boil it
  3. After heating, put in some honey
  4. If you have them, also put in some nuts
